About Us

One Facility.
Complete Capability.

SpiceVista Corporation is a professionally managed red chilli processing company headquartered in Bareilly, Uttar Pradesh — one of India's premier spice-growing regions.

We have built an integrated infrastructure that brings every stage of processing under one roof: from raw chilli intake and cold storage to AI-based optical sorting, stem cutting, grinding, and final packaging.

Under our flagship PL Brand, we deliver consistent quality products to domestic and export markets alike.
